Do I need a referral before booking?

Some patients are referred by a dentist or doctor, while others contact the practice directly. Calling ahead is the simplest way to confirm what is needed for your appointment.

Can adults visit for orthodontic care?

Yes. Orthodontic concerns affect children, teens, and adults, so a consultation can help determine the most appropriate next step for your situation.

What happens at a first appointment?

Patients can expect a conversation about their concerns, an assessment, and guidance on what further records or treatment planning may be recommended.
